    • Follow Me

    Released on January 2, 2023, this track featuring YBW is a declaration of Skex’s artistic identity and confidence. It stands out as one of his most-streamed tracks on Spotify and Apple Music, a testament to his clarity and presence in the music scene.

    Negus/Done

    Dropping on December 23, 2023, this track reflects on the influences of Skex’s upbringing and critiques societal flaws. It’s a raw exploration of how people can become trapped in a flawed system, drawing from the complexities of his own experiences.

    Highway

    One of Skex’s standout tracks from 2021, released September 7 with OGY KING, “Highway” showcases his playful side, inspired by Andre 3000’s flow. It’s a celebration of creativity and fun, resonating with fans for its infectious energy.

    Leaving the love island

    Released May 29, 2021, and featuring KXDZAII, this track captures the emotional aftermath of significant life events. Like “Highway,” it’s among Skex’s most popular releases from 2021, channeling the depth of his experiences into a poignant expression of personal growth.
