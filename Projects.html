    • Follow Me

    Follow Me was released in early 2023(on the second of January) and features YBW.This song is about him being very certain on who he is and what he is about as an artist. It is one of his mosted listened to songs on Spotify and Apple Music.

    Negus/Done

    Released on the 23rd of December 2023, NEGUS/DONE was inspired by the people Lil Skex knew and was surrouded by during his upbringing. He highlights the flaws "in the system" and how different people find themselves as slaves to it.

    Highway

    Highway is one of Lil Skex's most popular songs of 2021. It was released on the 7th of September 2021 featuring OGY KING. He was inspired by Andre 3000's flow and created the song off having fun.

    Leaving the love island

    Like Highway, this song was one of Lil Skex's most listened to projects on 2021. It was released on the 29th of May 2021 and features KXDZAII. He channeled the emotions that he felt after coming of out certain sitautions that had affected him. Leaving The Love Island was an expression of all that he felt.
